A Community Built Around Better Sleep.

We believe that understanding and improving our sleep is a fundamental part of a healthy life. NuYu is more than a device; it's a community-driven, open-source project for anyone who wants to build a better daily routine. Here, you can learn about our mission, our story, and how you can help shape the future of waking up.

Why We're Building in the Open

Accessibility

Great technology should be available to everyone. By developing NuYu as an open-source project, we are removing barriers and empowering people to build, modify, and learn from our work. A healthy circadian rhythm shouldn't have a paywall.

Collaboration

We don't have all the answers. The most powerful ideas come from collective passion. We invite developers, designers, sleep science enthusiasts, and makers to challenge our assumptions and contribute to a tool that is truly built by and for its community.

Transparency

In a world of closed-off technology, we choose a different path. From our circuit board schematics to the code that runs the alarm, everything is available for you to inspect. You have the right to understand and trust the technology in your home.

The Founder's Story

As a medical student, my life was a cycle of irregular sleep. Long hours of studying and clinical rotations made a consistent bedtime seem impossible. I found that it would take a long time to fix my sleep schedule, but once I was on it, maintaining other healthy habits like diet and exercise became easier. However, if I missed just a day or two, the entire structure would collapse, making it incredibly hard to get back on track.

I realized I needed more than just willpower; I needed a tool to help anchor my routine. Frustrated by the lack of comprehensive solutions, the idea for the NuYu clock was born—not as a product to sell, but as a project to solve a personal, yet universal, problem.
