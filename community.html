Why We're Building in the Open

Accessibility

Great technology should be available to everyone. By developing nuyu as an open-source project, we are removing barriers and empowering people to build, modify, and learn from our work. A healthy circadian rhythm shouldn't have a paywall.

Collaboration

We don't have all the answers. The most powerful ideas come from collective passion. We invite developers, designers, sleep science enthusiasts, and makers to challenge our assumptions and contribute to a tool that is truly built by and for its community.

Transparency

In a world of closed-off technology, we choose a different path. From our circuit board schematics to the code that runs the alarm, everything is available for you to inspect. You have the right to understand and trust the technology in your home.

Our Story: From Frustration to a Shared Mission

As a medical student, my life ran on an irregular schedule. The constant cycling between long study hours and clinical rotations meant that a 'normal' sleep pattern was a luxury. The real frustration wasn't just feeling tired; it was the slow, difficult process of clawing back to a stable routine, only to have it collapse after a single disruptive week.

I searched for a tool to help, but every solution felt like a piece of a larger puzzle. Standard alarms were just a shock to the system. Wellness apps demanded discipline when I had none left. Nothing treated the day as a wholeâ€”a connected cycle of waking, sleeping, and habit formation.

The idea for nuyu was born from that gap. It started as a personal project to build a single, comprehensive device that could anchor a routine using the principles of sleep science. But in sharing the concept, I realized this wasn't just my problemâ€”it was a shared struggle. That realization transformed nuyu from a personal fix into a community-driven, open-source project dedicated to building a better tool for everyone.
