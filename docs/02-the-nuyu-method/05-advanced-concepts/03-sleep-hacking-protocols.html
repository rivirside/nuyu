Behavioral Hacks

Mind Tricks That Work

The Military Method

Protocol: Progressive muscle relaxation + visualization

  1. Relax face muscles, including tongue and jaw
  2. Drop shoulders, then arms, one side at a time
  3. Exhale and relax chest, then legs
  4. Clear mind for 10 seconds
  5. Visualize calm scene or repeat "don't think"

Success rate: 96% after 6 weeks of practice

4-7-8 Breathing

Protocol: Parasympathetic activation through breath control

  • Exhale completely
  • Inhale through nose for 4 counts
  • Hold breath for 7 counts
  • Exhale through mouth for 8 counts
  • Repeat 3-4 cycles
Cognitive Shuffling

Protocol: Disrupt anxiety loops with random imagery

  • Pick a random word (e.g., "SLEEP")
  • For each letter, think of words starting with it
  • Visualize each word briefly
  • Move to next letter when stuck
