Your Day-by-Day Plan

1

Discovery Day

Let nuyu learn your baseline

Easy Start

Today's Goals:

Sleep normally - no changes to routine

Check morning insights (don't judge, just observe)

Note your subjective sleep quality (1-10)

What You'll Learn:

Your actual vs. perceived sleep time, number of awakenings, and basic sleep architecture. Don't worry if the data seems off—nuyu is still learning your patterns.

💡 Pro Tip: Baseline is Gold

Resist the urge to "sleep better" because you're being tracked. Normal nights provide the most valuable data for optimization.

2

Pattern Recognition

Understanding your sleep stages

Learning Mode

Today's Goals:

Explore the sleep stage breakdown in detail

Identify your deep sleep timing

Continue normal sleep routine

What You'll Learn:

The difference between light, deep, and REM sleep. When each stage typically occurs for you. Why you might feel groggy some mornings (hint: sleep inertia from deep sleep awakening).

3

Timing Insights

When you actually fall asleep

Discovery

Today's Goals:

Check your sleep latency (time to fall asleep)

Compare bedtime vs. actual sleep time

Notice any patterns in your data

What You'll Learn:

Your true sleep latency, optimal bedtime window, and whether you're giving yourself enough sleep opportunity. Many people discover they need to go to bed earlier than they thought.

💡 Pro Tip: The 3-Night Rule

After tonight, you have three nights of baseline data. This is when patterns start becoming visible and nuyu's recommendations become personalized.

4

First Optimization

Your initial improvement

Action Day

Today's Goals:

Implement ONE suggestion from nuyu

Set a consistent bedtime alarm

Try the smart alarm for tomorrow

What You'll Learn:

How small changes impact your sleep. The power of consistent timing. Why nuyu's suggestions are specific to YOUR patterns, not generic advice.

5

Recovery Focus

Understanding sleep quality

Deep Dive

Today's Goals:

Review your Recovery Score trends

Identify what impacts your score

Continue with consistent bedtime

What You'll Learn:

Recovery Score isn't just about duration—it factors in sleep efficiency, time in deep sleep, disruptions, and HRV. You'll see how behavior impacts recovery.

6

Environmental Audit

Optimizing your sleep space

Improvement

Today's Goals:

Check disruption patterns in your data

Make one environmental improvement

Test nuyu's environmental sensors

What You'll Learn:

How temperature, sound, and light affect your sleep. Whether your partner's movements wake you. If your room is optimized for deep sleep.

💡 Weekend Advantage

It's Saturday! Use the weekend to experiment with sleep timing. Try sleeping without an alarm to find your natural wake time.

7

Week Review & Planning

Insights and next steps

Milestone

Today's Goals:

Review your week's trends and insights

Identify your biggest sleep challenge

Set one goal for next week

What You'll Learn:

Your sleep personality profile, average sleep debt, consistency score, and personalized optimization priorities. nuyu now knows you well enough for advanced features.

Common Week 1 Challenges

It's Normal If You Experience:

📱 Data Obsession

Challenge: Checking the app multiple times per night

Solution: Put phone in airplane mode after checking evening insights. Trust nuyu to track without your supervision.

😟 Performance Anxiety

Challenge: Trying too hard to "sleep well" for good scores

Solution: Remember that variation is normal. Focus on trends over individual nights. Bad nights provide valuable data too.

🤔 Data Confusion

Challenge: Not understanding all the metrics yet

Solution: Focus on Sleep Score and one other metric this week. Understanding deepens with time. Use the in-app explanations.

⚡ Change Overwhelm

Challenge: Wanting to fix everything at once

Solution: Stick to ONE change at a time. Consistent bedtime alone can improve sleep by 23%. Build slowly.

Remember: Progress, Not Perfection

Your first week with nuyu is about discovery, not optimization. You're building a foundation of understanding that will support months and years of better sleep. Every night of data makes nuyu smarter and your insights deeper.

Some people see dramatic improvements in week 1. Others need 2-3 weeks to notice changes. Both paths are normal. Trust the process, stick with the basics, and let compound improvements work their magic.

You've taken the first steps on a journey that will transform not just your nights, but your days. Welcome to the nuyu family!
