The 15-Minute Setup

1

Charge Your nuyu (2 min)

Connect the magnetic charger. The LED will pulse orange while charging, turn solid green when ready. Even 10 minutes gives you a week of use!

2

Download the App (1 min)

Search "nuyu sleep" in your app store or scan the QR code on the welcome card. The app is free with no subscription required.

3

Create Your Profile (3 min)

Basic info: wake time goal, sleep challenges, and chronotype. Don't overthink it—nuyu learns and adapts as you use it.

4

Pair Your Device (1 min)

Press the nuyu button once. It'll pulse blue. The app will find it automatically. Accept the pairing request.

5

Place on Mattress (2 min)

Slide the strap under your mattress at chest level. Position nuyu on top, sensor side up. It should sit flat and secure.

6

Run Calibration (3 min)

Lie in your normal sleep position. Tap "Calibrate" in the app. Breathe normally for 2 minutes. nuyu learns your baseline.

7

Set First Goal (3 min)

Start simple: "Consistent bedtime" or "Track sleep quality." You can add more goals later. Tonight, just let nuyu learn.

Your First Night

  2. Check trends, not nights - Individual nights vary. Look for patterns over 5-7 days.
  3. Start with bedtime - If making changes, consistent bedtime is the highest impact.

Common Questions

Do I need to open the app before bed?

No! nuyu automatically detects when you go to bed and wake up. The app can be completely closed.

Will it work with my partner in bed?

Yes! nuyu uses advanced signal processing to isolate your biometrics from your partner's movements.

How accurate is it compared to a sleep lab?

nuyu achieves 94% accuracy compared to polysomnography for sleep stage detection. It's the most accurate non-wearable consumer device.

Do I need WiFi?

No, nuyu uses Bluetooth Low Energy. It stores up to 30 days of data locally if your phone isn't nearby.

Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

Not detecting sleep

Ensure nuyu is flat against the mattress, under your torso area. Re-run calibration if needed.

Won't pair

Turn Bluetooth off/on. Press nuyu button for 5 seconds to reset. Make sure no one else paired with it.

Missing data

Check that Bluetooth stays on overnight. nuyu stores data, so it'll sync when reconnected.

Battery draining fast

This is rare. Ensure firmware is updated in app settings. Contact support if under 3 weeks battery life.
