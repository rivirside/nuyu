Before You Begin

Most nuyu issues can be resolved in under 5 minutes. This guide is organized from quick fixes to advanced solutions. Start at the top—chances are your solution is in the first section.

🚀 Quick Fixes (Try These First!)

🔄

Restart Everything

Turn off nuyu (hold button 5 sec), force quit app, restart phone. Fixes 50% of issues.

🔋

Check Battery

Low battery can cause connectivity issues. Charge for 30 minutes and retry.

📱

Update App

Check app store for updates. We release fixes regularly.

📶

Bluetooth Toggle

Turn Bluetooth off for 10 seconds, then back on. Re-pair if needed.

📍

Check Placement

Ensure nuyu is flat on mattress, sensor side up, at chest level.

🛠️

Run Calibration

Settings → Device → Calibrate. Lie still for 2 minutes.

Common Issues & Solutions

😴 Sleep Detection Issues

Not detecting when I fall asleep

Common
1

Check placement - nuyu must be under your torso area, not under pillows or at foot of bed

2

Run calibration while lying in your normal sleep position

3

Ensure mattress strap is tight - device shouldn't move

4

Check sensitivity settings: Settings → Detection → Increase to "High"

Shows me awake when I was sleeping

Minor
1

This often happens with very still sleepers

2

Enable "Still Sleeper Mode" in Settings → Sleep Profile

3

nuyu will learn your patterns over 5-7 nights

Partner's movements affecting my data

Common
1

Enable "Partner Filter" in Settings → Advanced

2

Place nuyu closer to your side of bed

3

Run "Partner Calibration" with both in bed

📱 Connectivity Problems

nuyu won't connect to app

Urgent
1

Ensure nuyu is charged (LED should respond to button press)

2

Force quit app and reopen

3

Delete device from Bluetooth settings and re-pair

4

Reset nuyu: Hold button for 15 seconds until LED flashes red 3 times

Keeps disconnecting during the night

Common
1

Check phone's battery optimization settings - exclude nuyu app

2

Keep phone within 30 feet of nuyu device

3

Disable "Bluetooth Scanning" in phone's location settings

4

Update device firmware: Settings → Device → Check for Updates

📊 Data & Accuracy Issues

Sleep stages seem wrong

Minor
1

nuyu needs 3-5 nights to learn your patterns

2

Ensure device hasn't shifted position

3

Check for mattress compatibility: very soft or very firm may need adjustment

4

Enable "Learning Mode" for 1 week: Settings → AI → Enhanced Learning

Missing data for entire night

Urgent
1

Check if nuyu was in range - it stores 30 days locally

2

Force sync: Pull down on main screen to refresh

3

Check device memory: Settings → Device → Storage (clear if full)

4

Contact support - we may be able to recover data

Understanding Error Codes

LED Flash Patterns & Meanings

3 Red Flashes Low battery (charge immediately)
5 Red Flashes Sensor error (clean sensor, recalibrate)
Continuous Red Critical error (factory reset required)
Blue-Red Alternate Firmware update in progress (don't disconnect)
Green-Blue Alternate Pairing mode active

Factory Reset (Last Resort)

⚠️Factory Reset Instructions

Warning: This erases all device data. Your historical data in the app is safe.

  1. Open app and go to Settings → Device → Advanced
  2. Tap "Factory Reset" and confirm
  3. Hold nuyu button for 20 seconds until LED flashes rainbow pattern
  4. Release button - device will restart
  5. Re-pair device and run initial setup

When to use: Only after trying all other solutions or if directed by support.

Preventive Maintenance

Keep your nuyu running smoothly:

  • Monthly: Clean sensor with soft, dry cloth
  • Monthly: Check for app and firmware updates
  • Quarterly: Re-run calibration
  • Yearly: Replace mattress strap if worn
