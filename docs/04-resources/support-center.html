Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How long does it take to see improvements in my sleep? ▼

Most users see initial insights within 3-5 nights as nuyu learns your patterns. Meaningful improvements typically begin within 2 weeks, with significant transformations occurring over 1-3 months. Remember, sustainable change takes time!

Do I need to wear anything to track my sleep? ▼

No! nuyu uses contactless sensing technology. Simply place it on your mattress under the fitted sheet. No wearables, no charging wearables, no discomfort—just natural sleep while nuyu does the work.

Will nuyu work with my partner in bed? ▼

Yes! nuyu's advanced algorithms can distinguish between you and your partner. For best results, place nuyu on your side of the bed. Partner Mode in settings helps filter out their movements for even more accurate tracking.

Technical Questions

How often do I need to charge nuyu? ▼

nuyu's battery lasts up to 3 months on a single charge! The app will notify you when battery is low (with about a week remaining). Charging takes 2-3 hours via the included USB-C cable.

What happens if my phone isn't nearby? ▼

nuyu stores up to 30 days of data locally. When your phone reconnects via Bluetooth, all data syncs automatically. You won't lose any sleep tracking, even if your phone is off or in another room.

Is my sleep data private and secure? ▼

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted end-to-end and never shared without your explicit consent. You own your data and can export or delete it anytime. We're HIPAA compliant and take privacy seriously. Read our full privacy policy at nuyu.com/privacy.

Subscription & Warranty

Do I need a subscription to use nuyu? ▼

No subscription required! All core features are free forever: sleep tracking, insights, smart alarm, and basic analytics. nuyu Pro ($9.99/month) adds advanced features like AI coaching, unlimited experiments, and API access—but it's completely optional.

What warranty does nuyu come with? ▼

Every nuyu device includes a 2-year warranty covering defects and malfunctions. We also offer a 60-night sleep trial—if you're not sleeping better, return it for a full refund. Extended warranty and accident protection available at checkout.
