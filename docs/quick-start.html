Pro Placement Tip

Position your nuyu device 2-3 feet from your head and slightly to one side. This creates optimal scent diffusion without being overwhelming. Think of it as your "sleep soundtrack"‚Äîpresent but not intrusive.

2

Find Your Device's Perfect Home

Location is everything. Your nuyu device needs to be close enough to work its magic, but positioned to become part of your bedroom's peaceful atmosphere.

The Sweet Spot Checklist:

  • Height: At or slightly below head level when lying down
  • Distance: 2-3 feet from your pillow (close enough to smell, far enough to avoid overwhelming)
  • Stability: Stable surface that won't vibrate or tip
  • Access: Easy to reach for adjustments, but not so close you'll fidget with it
  • Airflow: Not blocked by walls or furniture‚Äîscent needs room to circulate

Avoid These Common Mistakes

We've seen thousands of setups. Here's what NOT to do:

  • Placing it directly next to your head (too intense)
  • Hiding it in a corner (scent won't reach you)
  • Putting it on an unstable surface (you'll worry about it falling)
  • Blocking the vents with other objects

Power Up & Wake Up Your Device

This is where the magic begins. Your device will guide you through a simple setup that creates your personalized sleep profile.

  1. Connect the power: Plug the USB-C cable into the back of the device, then into the wall adapter
  2. First boot sequence: The display will illuminate with a gentle glow‚Äîthis is your device coming to life
  3. Language selection: Choose your preferred language (you can change this later)
  4. Time zone setup: Critical for syncing with your local sunrise/sunset
  5. Wi-Fi connection (optional): Enables app features and automatic updates
