Troubleshooting Common Challenges

Look, I'm not going to pretend this journey is always smooth. You're going to hit some bumps, and that's completely normal. In fact, if you don't encounter any resistance, you're probably not pushing hard enough.

Here are the three most common challenges I see, along with exactly how to handle them:

"I'm still tired even with more sleep"

Focus on consistency over duration. It's better to get 6.5 hours at the same time every night than to fluctuate between 5 and 9 hours.

"I can't fall asleep at the earlier bedtime"

This is normal! Your circadian rhythm is adjusting. Try reducing the shift to 10 minutes instead of 15, and ensure you're getting bright light in the morning.

"Weekend social events disrupt my schedule"

Plan for this! If you must stay up late, try to wake up no more than 1 hour later than usual, then get back on track the next night.
