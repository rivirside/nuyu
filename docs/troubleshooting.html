Troubleshooting Guide

Solutions to common issues and questions

Device Issues

Device won't turn on

  • Check power cable connection at both ends
  • Try a different outlet
  • Hold power button for 10 seconds for hard reset
  • Check if power adapter LED is lit

Scent not diffusing

  • Ensure cartridge is properly inserted (should click)
  • Check if cartridge is empty (indicator on display)
  • Clean diffusion vents with dry cloth
  • Verify intensity setting isn't at 0%

Display too bright/dim

  • Adjust brightness in settings menu
  • Enable auto-brightness for time-based adjustment
  • Check if night mode is active

Sleep Issues

Still having trouble falling asleep

  • Increase evening scent intensity by 10%
  • Start wind-down routine 45 minutes before bed
  • Ensure room temperature is 65-68Â°F
  • Try chamomile instead of lavender

Waking up groggy

  • Enable Smart Wake feature
  • Increase morning light intensity
  • Add 15 minutes to sleep duration
  • Check if waking during deep sleep phase

Partner has different sleep schedule

  • Use directional scent mode
  • Position device on your side of bed
  • Use earbuds for audio alarms
  • Consider separate devices

Maintenance

Cleaning your device

  • Wipe exterior weekly with damp cloth
  • Clean vents monthly with compressed air
  • Never submerge in water
  • Replace filters every 6 months

Cartridge care

  • Store unused cartridges in cool, dark place
  • Don't shake or invert cartridges
  • Use within 6 months of opening
  • Dispose of empty cartridges responsibly
