Product & Features

The nuyu system is designed to release scents at a low, sub-arousal threshold. The olfactory system is unique in that it can process these cues during sleep without causing arousal, unlike a loud noise. The intensity is calibrated to be just enough for the brain to process, but not enough to disturb your sleep.

You can use any essential oil or fragrance oil of your choice. The device is designed to be compatible with standard oils available from any retailer. While we may offer our own curated scents in the future, you are never locked into buying from us. The goal is to give you maximum flexibility.
