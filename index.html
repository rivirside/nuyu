The Science Behind NuYu

Olfactory conditioning is a powerful tool that leverages the sense of smell to influence behavior. By associating specific scents with particular times of day, we can train our bodies to respond in desired ways, reinforcing your natural circadian rhythm.

How Scents Influence Sleep and Wakefulness

The scents we choose are selected based on scientific research into their effects on the human body and mind.

  • For sleep, we use calming scents like lavender and chamomile. Studies have shown these can improve sleep quality by promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety.
  • For wakefulness, we use invigorating scents like citrus and peppermint. These scents are known to stimulate the senses, increase energy levels, and improve mood.

By associating these scents with specific times of day, our diffuser helps to create a consistent routine that supports your circadian rhythm.

Our Story: A Personal Quest for Better Sleep

As a medical student, my life was a cycle of irregular sleep. Long hours of studying and clinical rotations made a consistent bedtime seem impossible. I found that it would take a long time to fix my sleep schedule, but once I was on it, maintaining other healthy habits like diet and exercise became easier. However, if I missed just a day or two, the entire structure would collapse, making it incredibly hard to get back on track.

I realized I needed more than just willpower; I needed a tool to help anchor my routine. Frustrated by the lack of comprehensive solutions, the idea for the NuYu clock was born—not as a product to sell, but as a project to solve a personal, yet universal, problem.
