From Research to a Real-World Solution

Understanding the science is the first step. The next is applying it. Here’s how the nuyu project translates these powerful scientific principles into tangible features designed to help you.

Engineering for Your Circadian Rhythm

The science shows that light is a primary regulator of your internal clock. That's why nuyu features a sunrise-simulating light to naturally boost morning cortisol and a time-based night light to support evening melatonin production.

A Toolkit for Olfactory Entrainment

Targeted Memory Reactivation requires distinct cues. That's why nuyu has two independent wells for separate "Wake" and "Sleep" scents, allowing you to build the associative links needed to reinforce your habits while you sleep.

Personalized for Your Biology

Chronotype matters for complex habits. Take our validated quiz to help you understand your unique sleep profile and tailor your routines for maximum effectiveness.

The Scientific Pillars of nuyu

Our approach is an integration of four distinct but interconnected fields of science. Explore them to understand how nuyu works.

The Science of Sleep

Sleep isn't just rest; it's a critical maintenance period where the brain cleanses itself, consolidates memories, and regulates emotions.

The Science of Habit

Habits form by offloading tasks to the brain's automatic systems. Leveraging natural rhythms, like the morning cortisol peak, can significantly accelerate this process.

The Science of Scent

Smell has a unique 'superhighway' to the brain's emotion and memory centers, making it an ideal tool to create powerful associations for new behaviors.

The nuyu Synthesis

We integrate these principles into a 24-hour system, using scent to link waking habits with nighttime memory consolidation (TMR) for accelerated learning.
